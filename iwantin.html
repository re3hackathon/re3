Creative storytellers

Crucial stories deserve top talent.

We know you are really busy. We know you are really talented. This is a chance to work on something you feel passionate about, meet and work with talented collaborators, and make something meaningful.

If you're as excited as we are about this, all you need to do right now is express your interest in the form below.

Sign up now indicate to indicate your interest in crafting beautiful stories at the Re3 StoryHack in New York City, from October 4 - 6th.

Send it!

Creative people of all stripes are welcome. Just let us know what skills you can bring to the table and why this thing shouldn't happen without you. In mid September, we'll select 75 creative story hackers and ask you to commit to participating during the Re3 Story Hack weekend in New York City. Story hackers will be organized into curated teams based on everyone's interests and expertise to ensure all teams are well balanced. Each team will tackle one of ten selected stories spanning a wide range of social issues. Selected stories and their briefs will be revealed in mid September.

We know you might find the typical hackathon a bit chaotic and ineffective, but we’re here to change that. We want to add a bit more clarity to the process so we all know why we're sacrificing our weekends. From the moment you arrive at the Re3 StoryHack, you will have a clear creative brief to work with and you will have had a chance to connect with your teammates. That means you'll be able to get right into making something brilliant. Advisors, subject matter experts, and creative directors will be available for check-ins while you work. It's going to be awesome!
