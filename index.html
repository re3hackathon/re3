About

The most important issues deserve the best storytellers.

React to the world around you. Rethink the way important issues are communicated. Resolve confusion about some of today's greatest challenges.The Re3 StoryHack is an experiment, but damn, it’s an important one. It’s time to expand the boundaries of justice-focused persuasive storytelling.

We’re asking changemakers to propose specific stories relating to complex issues like economic fairness, climate change, educational opportunity, and many more.

We’re offering creative storytellers the chance to choose one of ten selected stories and work with top-notch teammates. Over the Re3 StoryHack weekend, we will innovate new ways of thinking and communicating these stories in language; written, visualized, performed, coded and more.

An interactive exhibit? Excellent. Flash mob? Sure. App? Why not. Sculpture? Perfect. Print piece? Absolutely. There is absolutely no limitation of medium for the teams’ final outputs as long as they tell captivating, powerful stories.

Changemakers and creative storytellers with a conscience, start your engines.

The Issues

Let’s tackle the big issues of our time and amplify the voices seeking to make this world more just. Let’s challenge the powers that be!

Why Storytelling

The Re3 StoryHack is an experiment in pushing the boundaries of justice-based persuasive storytelling.

Let’s be honest. We all have issues that we care about, and that we’d like others to also care about. While it’s easy to march out an army of facts and hope that they overwhelm people into thinking differently, that usually isn’t enough to change hearts and minds.

  • 1
    We won't preach to the choir.

    Let’s articulate why these stories matter to those who think they don’t.

  • 2
    We’ll start with empathy.

    Let’s listen and try to understand different perspectives.

  • 3
    We’ll find the human side to the facts.

    Let’s be rigorous, but always put people before numbers.

  • 4
    We’ll tell beautiful stories.

    Stories that people different from us can connect to, relate to, and be open to.

  • 5
    We’ll pull out all the stops.

    We’ll use humor, love, metaphor, sex, and aything under the sun so our stories are heard.
