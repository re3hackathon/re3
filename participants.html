Over 200 creatives applied to participate at Re3 StoryHack and join us in our experiment for persuasive justice-based storytelling. About 100 creatives were selected and matched with the stories they felt most passionate about. The caliber of talent, range of experience skills and passion for meaningful storytelling among you is truly mind-blowing and we can't wait to have you all in a room together. We have Emmy-winning filmmakers, top developers and designers, students from Parsons, SVA, NYU and Yale and brilliant people from major organizations like the New York Times, Google, NBC News, Wall Street Journal, Fast Company, Channel Thirteen, DesigNYC and Bloomberg, all dedicating their weekends to craft these important stories!

We can't wait to see what you create! Here are the teams organized by story.
