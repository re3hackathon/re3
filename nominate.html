Nominate

This is your megaphone.

You are a changemaker in a non-profit fighting an injustice. You are an author, journalist or academic who’d like to find new ways of expressing the ideas you’re passionate about. Whatever your social cause, whatever your story, if it’s something people will care about, we’d like to hear it and help you express it in new ways! We are not here to figure out how to help any one organization market themselves. We’re looking for stories that haven’t been told, or haven’t been told well –the stories people should be paying attention to, but aren’t just yet.

See some sample story ideas and learn more about the kinds of stories we’re looking for.

We’ll be in touch shortly after you submit your story with any questions we might need answered to clarify your idea. We’ll then assist ten selected finalists in developing detailed creative briefs for our creative storytellers to work with. Learn more about what role you could play if your idea is selected.

All you need to do now is click the button below to email us a brief description of your story idea. Try to keep it short and sweet, and let us know why you feel it’s important.
