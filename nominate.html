Nominate Awesome Stories

This is your megaphone.

Maybe you're a changemaker in a non-profit fighting an injustice. Maybe you're an author, journalist or academic who’d like to find new ways of expressing the ideas you’re passionate about. Maybe you're a David trying to stare down a Goliath. Whatever your cause, if it’s something people will care about, we’d like to hear it and help you express it in new ways! We’re looking for stories that haven’t been told, or haven’t been told well – the stories people will be paying attention to after the Story Hack!

See some sample story ideas and learn more about the kinds of stories we want to tackle at the Re3 StoryHack.

All you need to do now is click the button below to email us a few lines describing your story idea. Try to keep it short and sweet, and let us know why you feel it’s important.

Tell us your story idea!





We’ll be in touch shortly after you share your idea to provide you with feedback. Your next step will be to fill out a Story Brief that gives us more detail about your story. Story Briefs are due by September 10th. The Re3 Crew will judge the briefs and select ten finalists in mid September. We will then help the finalists polish their briefs and get them ready for the Re3 StoryHack weekend. If you'd like to jump straight to filling out the Story Brief, be our guest. Otherwise, just use the button above to tell us what your story is about in a few sentences. We'll give you some feedback before you fill out your Story Brief.
