Show and Tell

Re3 StoryHack Show and Tell - October 5th 4PM

Please join us for Show and Tell of Stories.

This weekend, 15-20 teams of creative professionals are coming together to craft products that will shift attitudes about some of the most crucial issues of our time - issues of social justice and human rights that affect us all.

Our teams of Emmy-winning filmmakers, top developers and designers, students from Parsons, SVA, NYU and Yale and brilliant people from the New York Times, Google, NBC News, Wall Street Journal, Fast Company, Channel Thirteen, DesigNYC, Bloomberg and many more, will be presenting the fruits of their labor in front of our panel of judges.

10 stories that matter + 100 creative professionals = magic waiting to happen

We invite you to join us for an afternoon that promises to be exciting, inspiring, thought-provoking, and change-making. Hope to see you soon !
