There are a few roles we need for every team

Social issue based stories can be submitted by anyone. If your story is chosen and developed into a full cretive brief, here are a few roles you could play during the Re3 StoryHack weekend:

  • The subject matter expert – Someone who can lay out the big-picture context for an issue, inspire people to care about it. Subject matter experts have deep knowledge of relevant facts, and can provide rigorous reality checks for the creatives.
  • The audience surrogate – someone who knows the target audience really well, or who can access them easily.
  • The story evangelist – someone who will take the story that’s produced and promote it incessantly.

    • Creative storytelling teams will be made up of the following roles:

  • The team leader – someone with great leadership, creative vision and project management skills. You are a decisionmaker, and unifier. You know how to keep creatives focused, motivated and moving forward.
  • The creative storyteller – someone who knows how to convey stories in compelling and unexpected ways that form memorable impressions. You can be a designer, developers, data scientists, filmmakers, editors, writers, illustrators , animators, or anything else we haven't thought of.
  • The data scientist – an analyst who can detect patterns and trends from complex databases in a magic-like manner. Data scientists can connect big numbers to big ideas.
