There are a few roles we need for every team

Social issue based stories can be submitted by anyone. But we’ll be looking to make teams where each of the following roles is filled – sometimes one person can fill multiple roles:

  • The subject matter exper – Someone who knows the information/facts deeply, so that the story reflects a rigorous reality.
  • The analyst – a data scientist who draw out patterns and trends from complex facts, and perform magic trick analyses to make big numbers and ideas more manageable.
  • The story-creator – someone who knows how to create stories that stick with people, to find new ways of looking at something, to use character and plot and metaphor and visualisation and humour to grab people’s attention.
  • The team manager – someone who keeps the teams focused and moving forward.
  • The audience surrogate – someone who knows the target audience really well, or who can access them easily.
  • The story evangelist – someone who will take the story that’s produced and promote it incessantly.
  • The story creators – designers, developers, filmmakers and editors, cartoonists – people who will create and craft the actual story.
