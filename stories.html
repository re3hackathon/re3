The Stories

These are the stories we'll be working on.

A total of 30 stories were nominated by changemakers for the Re3 StoryHack. Each changemaker put in considerable effort to complete our Story Brief and many committed additional time to working through the details of their story with us on the phone. The Re3 crew would like to send a huge thank you to everyone for believing in this process!

After a thorough review process, the following 10 stories have been selected for creative teams to tackle during the Re3 StoryHack.

1. Story Title

The Issue:

The man in the mirror: Changing perceptions about what it takes for our nation’s poor to get ahead.

Why is your story important?

Studies and stats on justice are unequivocal - the criminal/jail system in the US doesn't do what it's meant to - it doesn't deter potential criminals, it doesn't make victims feel better, and it doesn't reform those convicted, it hardens them.
